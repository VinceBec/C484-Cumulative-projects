Acclaim

Catch-22 was ranked by TIME magazine among the top 100 english language modern novels.
Number 11 in a web poll by the BBC's Big Read of the UK's best-loved books.
The Modern Library ranked it 7th among other books such as The Great Gatsby and 1984 in the 100 best novels of all time.

In 1970, the novel recived a film adaptation of the same name. The novel would also get a pilot episode for a comedy in 1973.
Just recently in May of 2019, Hulu released a six-episode miniseries based on the novel and produced by and starring George Clooney.
Back in 2011, C-SPAN released a panel and short reading on Catch-22.
